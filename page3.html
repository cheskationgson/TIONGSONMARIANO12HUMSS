Hello everyone! Here is a look at the items contained in my pencil case! All of these items are Japanese brands, as I find that the most worth-buying stationery are those made from Japan!

I bought the pencil case from MUJI. I never expected it to be my go-to pencil case! I like this much more than my Lihit Lab Double Teffa Pencase (which I ended up using as a travel pencil case). The mesh easily lets you see through the contents of your pencil case. It’s cylindrical shape makes it stay compact despite being filled completely.

To start, here are the items I currently have in my pencil case:

External Accessories:
- Bakugo Katsuki Keychain from @yukiorequiem – I got this keychain from Cosplay Mania 2018 from the Artists’ Booth
- Aoba Johsai (Seijoh) Enamel Pin from @plupip.art – I got this pin from Cosplay Mania 2019 from the Artists’ Booth as well
- Singularity Yeontan Enamel Pin from @candarella – I also got this one from Cosplay Mania 2019 in the same booths section
- Meatball Bakugo Enamel Pin from @plupip.art – I got this one with the Aoba Johsai enamel pin 
Non-Writing Equipments:
- SunStar Stickyle Scissors – A less bulky version of scissors. Very compact and sharp despite its size.
- Pilot G Neox Graphite HB Mechanical Pencil Lead (0.5) – I personally don’t like this one. It’s too chalky and dark for me. But I got it and there’s 40 pieces of it so I’m not gonna let it go to waste.
- Tombow Mono Eraser in black and white – A real, high quality eraser. I use the white one for erasing light writings, while black for the heavy duty erases.
- ReWrite Correction Tape – I don’t like the quality of this one considering it’s pretty generic, but I have no other correction tape and I had this one at home.
- Compact Mini Stapler from MUJI – This is such a convenient stapler. Really compact but it does its job well. It can’t hold a really thick number of paper but for something to use for emergencies, it’s pretty well-made.
- Acrylic 15 cm Ruler from MUJI – Very convenient to have, and small enough to throw into your pencil case.


Writing Equipment:
- Smooth Capped Gel Ink Pen (0.38 and 0.5) from MUJI – Hands down my most favorite pen. It dries a bit slow but it doesn’t bother me even though I’m left handed.
- Uni Style Fit Customizable Multi-Pen – I filled it with Uniball Signo ink. A bit pricey but worth the quality.
- Pilot Hi-Tec C Coleto Customizable Multi-Pen
- Pentel i+ Customizable Multi-Pen – Containing Pentel Sliccies Ink Refill
- Kokuyo Beetle Tip Soft Color Dual Tip Highlighter
- Window Highlighter from MUJI
- Pilot DR. Grip Mechanical Pencil – A top mechanical pencil favorite for me. The most ergonomic one out there.
- Uni Alpha Gel Slim Mechanical Pencil – A really squishy grip.
- Uni Alpha Gel Shaker Mechanical Pencil – A shaker pen with a really squishy grip.
- Tombow Olno Body Knock Mechanical Pencil – Bends in the middle in order to dispense lead.
- Zebra Delguard Mechanical Pencil
- Uni Kuru Toga Mechanical Pencil

You can see that I have a lot of mechanical pencils. I really like to use mechanical pencils, so I only use my pens when required to. But other than that, my go-to writing equipment are always mechanical pencils.
